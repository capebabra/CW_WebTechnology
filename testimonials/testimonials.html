Question & Answer

  • What materials are used to build wooden houses?

  • We use only high-quality wood: glued laminated timber, solid wood and other environmentally friendly materials that ensure durability and thermal insulation of the house.

  • Is it possible to change the house design to suit my wishes?

  • Yes, we offer custom design, taking into account all your wishes, including layout, style and additional elements.

  • How long does it take to build a wooden house?

  • On average, construction takes from 3 to 6 months, depending on the complexity of the project and weather conditions.

  • Do you provide a warranty on your homes?

  • Of course! We offer installation of automation systems, including lighting, heating and security control, for maximum comfort.
